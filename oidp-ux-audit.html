HHS Office of Infectious Disease and HIV/AIDS Policy UX Audit

A USWDS-based, WCAG 2.1 AA-ready audit interface for reviewing accessibility, content clarity, user journeys, and redesign opportunities for hhs.gov/oidp.

Audit scope: OIDP landing page, initiatives, national action plans, advisory committees, and common public-information tasks.

Audit snapshot

WCAG 2.1 AA target

Primary finding

The OIDP experience is visually clean but asks visitors to decode federal terminology, recover from dead ends, and remember acronyms. The highest-impact redesign work is clearer information scent, persistent topic navigation, accessible search and filtering, and better external-link expectations.
